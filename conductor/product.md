# Initial Concept

The user wants to build a Shoppable Video Accelerator that uses Gemini to intelligently map products to video creative, automatically identifying relevant merchandise within video content to ensure accurate and engaging ad placements.

# Product Definition

## Vision

To revolutionize video advertising by transforming passive video views into interactive shopping experiences, enabling retailers to scale shoppable video ads effortlessly using AI.

## Target Audience

- **Large Retailers:** Companies with extensive product catalogs and high volumes of video creative.
- **Marketing Teams:** Professionals looking to increase the performance and ROI of their Demand Gen campaigns.
- **E-commerce Managers:** Those responsible for maintaining accurate product feeds and ad placements.

## Core Goals

- **Automate Video Tagging:** Replace time-consuming manual processes with AI-driven product identification.
- **Improve Ad Relevance:** Ensure products shown in ads are semantically matched to the retailer's actual inventory.
- **Seamless Integration:** Connect directly with Google Merchant Center and Google Ads for an end-to-end workflow.
- **Scalability:** Leverage Google Cloud to handle thousands of videos and millions of products efficiently.

## Key Features

- **AI Video Analysis:** Gemini-powered analysis of video content to detect products.
- **Semantic Product Matching:** Vector Search and Embeddings to match identified products with SKU-level precision.
- **Merchant Center Integration:** Automated retrieval of product data via BigQuery Data Transfer.
- **Google Ads Integration:** Automated addition of relevant products to Demand Gen campaigns.
- **Centralized Dashboard:** A web application for managing videos, reviewing AI matches, and monitoring campaign performance.
- **Infrastructure as Code:** Fully automated deployment using Terraform.
